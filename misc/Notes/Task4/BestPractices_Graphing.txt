Good graphs vs bad

>Best practices
	>Problem: data are meaningless
	>Solution: do good science

	>Problem: smoothing is misleading
	>Solution: be judicious with smoothing; have theory to explain smooth

	>Problem: observations not stated
	>Solution: state observations or show them

	>Problem: should plot exist
	>Solution: show simple data w/ table or text

	>Problem: poor resolution
	>Solution: save graphs to whatever size you expect to use them in
